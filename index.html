ABOUT ME

Luxe Interior Design by MG is a luxury design firm that offers a full range of creative services rooted in passion, artistry, and inspired design. The studio works across ranges of projects, both residential and commercial, always collaborating closely with clients to capture their unique aesthetic while reflecting their individual lifestyle and needs.

Throughout my career, I have completed a wide range of projects, from full renovations to complete furnishing and styling, always approaching each space with creativity, precision, and a deep appreciation for aesthetics. My education in both graphic and interior design allows me to approach every project with a refined understanding of color, composition, and proportion.

I believe that every home or business has its own story to tell, a story that deserves to be expressed as a work of art. With this philosophy, I approach each project as a one of a kind creation, blending creativity, sophistication, and purpose.

Influenced by world travel, culture, fashion, and design, both classic and contemporary, I bring a refined yet adventurous sensibility to every space. The result is a thoughtfully curated environment that not only reflects my clients' personalities but also leaves a lasting impression.
